About Molecule Mutation Generator

An educational chemistry tool for visualizing how structural changes affect reactivity, mechanisms, and molecular properties.

What this site does

The Molecule Mutation Generator lets you describe a base molecule and a mutation, then uses AI to provide a qualitative analysis of how that change may affect:

  • Reactivity and likely mechanisms (e.g., SN1 vs SN2, E1 vs E2)
  • Acidity and basicity trends
  • Steric and electronic effects
  • Stability of intermediates and transition states
  • IUPAC-style naming suggestions and approximate structures
